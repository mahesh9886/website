About Us

Satyukt is an initiative by a pool of multidisciplinary technical professionals and advisors who have vast scientific experience gathered over decades by working on diverse problems and providing leading-edge solutions in India and abroad. Our vision is to provide big data analytics and insights for optimal decision making.

At Satyukt, we have considered the various available earth-observation satellite constellations who can provide insights for optimal decision making and have developed the innovative algorithms. The algorithms combine the multi-satellite data and enable us to develop the space-products such as agro-hydrological variables (i.e. soil moisture/water stress, crop health monitoring, likely crop yield, crop water requirements, crop water usage and more) at a farm-scale on a daily frequency. Satyukt is the only company in India which has expertise in understanding, processing, analyzing and interpreting the microwave/SAR satellite/sensor data.
